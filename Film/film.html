My Kind of Film

Movies & Series That Stole My Heart

A collection of my top movies and TV series that have left a lasting impression on me. From edge of your seat thrillers and epic adventures to heartwarming dramas and laugh out loud comedies, these are the stories that I keep coming back to.

TV Shows

The 100

Release Date: March 19, 2014

Nearly a century after a nuclear apocalypse wipes out most of humanity, the last survivors live aboard a massive space station called the Ark. With resources running dangerously low, 100 teenage prisoners are sent to Earth as test subjects to determine if the planet is habitable again.

Dare Devil

Release Date: April 10, 2015

After losing his sight in a childhood accident, Matt develops superhuman senses that allow him to "see" the world in a unique way. By day, he works as a defense attorney, but at night, he takes justice into his own hands, battling criminals and corrupt figures that the legal system cannot stop.

Click to watch the trailer.

The Night Agent

Release Date: March 23, 2023

The Night Agent is a gripping political thriller about FBI agent Peter Sutherland, who answers a mysterious call that pulls him into a deadly conspiracy within the U.S. government. Teaming up with tech entrepreneur Rose Larkin, he must uncover the truth while staying ahead of dangerous enemies. Packed with suspense, action, and twists, it's a must-watch for thriller fans!

Click to watch the trailer.

The Punisher

Release Date: November 17, 2017

The Punisher is a dark and intense Marvel series that follows Frank Castle, a former Marine seeking vengeance for the brutal murder of his family. After uncovering a larger conspiracy tied to his past, he becomes The Punisher, a relentless vigilante who takes down criminals with brutal force.

Click to watch the trailer.

Sex Education

Release Date: January 11, 2019

Sex Education is a bold and hilarious coming-of-age series that follows Otis Milburn, a socially awkward teenager with a sex therapist mother. Despite his own inexperience, he teams up with rebellious classmate Maeve Wiley to run an underground sex therapy clinic at their school, helping students navigate their personal and romantic struggles.

Movies

Avengers: End Game

Release Date: April 26, 2019

Avengers: Endgame is the epic conclusion to the Infinity Saga in the Marvel Cinematic Universe. After Thanos wipes out half of all life with the Infinity Gauntlet, the remaining Avengers—Iron Man, Captain America, Thor, Hulk, Black Widow, and Hawkeye—must find a way to undo the devastation.

The Amazing
Spider-Man

Release Date: June 29, 2012

The Amazing Spider-Man (2012) is a fresh take on the Spider-Man story, starring Andrew Garfield as Peter Parker. After discovering secrets about his parents' mysterious disappearance, Peter is bitten by a genetically modified spider, gaining incredible abilities.

Extraction

Release Date: April 24, 2020

Extraction is a high-octane action thriller starring Chris Hemsworth as Tyler Rake, a fearless black-market mercenary. Tasked with rescuing Ovi Mahajan, the kidnapped son of an international crime lord, Tyler navigates the dangerous streets of Bangladesh, battling ruthless enemies and corrupt forces.

13 Hours: The Secret Soldiers of Benghazi

Release Date: January 13, 2016

13 Hours: The Secret Soldiers of Benghazi is an intense war thriller based on true events. It follows a team of six elite military contractors who defend a U.S. diplomatic compound in Benghazi, Libya, after a deadly terrorist attack on September 11, 2012.

Click to watch the trailer.

World War Z

Release Date: June 20, 2013

World War Z is a thrilling apocalyptic action film starring Brad Pitt as Gerry Lane, a former UN investigator who embarks on a global mission to stop a fast-spreading zombie pandemic. As civilization collapses, Gerry races against time to find the origin of the outbreak and a possible cure, traveling through infected zones like South Korea, Israel, and Wales.
